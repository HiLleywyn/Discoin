# Discoin Wiki

Discoin is a Discord bot that turns a server into a living, simulated crypto
economy. Players trade tokens across a dozen networks, mine, stake,
run validators, provide liquidity, gamble, fish, raid dungeons, raise
companions, and climb a job ladder -- all from chat commands. Prices drift
continuously, AI market makers trade alongside players, and every action is
written to an on-chain transaction ledger.

This wiki is the player and operator handbook. Pick a starting point below.

## New here?

Start with **[Getting Started](Getting-Started)** -- it walks you through your
first balance, your first job, your first trade, and how the in-bot `,help`
system works. Then read **[Economy](Economy)** to understand wallets, banking,
daily rewards, and the job ladder.

> All actions use a text prefix (this wiki writes it as `,`). Your server's
> prefix is set by its admins and may differ -- check `,help` in your server.
> Slash (`/`) commands are informational only.
