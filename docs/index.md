# Discoin

A Discord economy bot with simulated crypto markets, mining, staking, AMM liquidity pools, lending, smart contracts, gambling, and a web dashboard.

---

## What is Discoin?

Discoin turns your Discord server into a fully simulated crypto economy. Players trade 40 built-in tokens across 12 networks, mine SUN and MTA with virtual rigs, stake tokens with player-run validators, provide liquidity to AMM pools, take out loans, gamble, and more.

Prices drift in real time via Geometric Brownian Motion. AI market makers trade automatically. All transactions produce hashes and are bundled into ledger blocks every 30 minutes.

## Features

- **Crypto market** -- 40 built-in tokens across 12 networks with real-time price drift
- **Trading** -- buy, sell, swap through AMM liquidity pools
- **Mining** -- GPU/ASIC rigs, solo/pool/group modes, Moneta-style difficulty
- **Staking** -- delegate to validators, earn hourly yield
- **PoS Validators** -- players run validators, process mempool, earn gas fees
- **Lending** -- borrow USD against crypto collateral with auto-liquidation
- **Savings** -- deposit USD for variable interest
- **Smart contracts** -- limit orders, escrow, vesting schedules
- **Gambling** -- coinflip, dice, blackjack, slots, roulette, mines
- **DeFi wallets** -- network-scoped wallets with cross-chain transfers
- **Shop & items** -- Hashstone, Lockstone, Vaultstone, Charms
- **AI moderation** -- scam/phishing detection with auto-timeout
- **Economy security monitor** -- passive abuse detection with AI alerts
- **Reports & bounties** -- player bug reports with admin rewards
- **Web dashboard** -- full trading, portfolio, charts, admin panel
- **REST API** -- 17 endpoint domains with themed Swagger UI
